Welcome to the Guessing Game!

This is a simple guessing game.
A random number between 1 and 100 has been selected.
You have 15 guess to guess the number!

After each guess, you will be told if your guess was too high or too low.
The game ends when you either guess the number or run out of guesses!

Everyones favorite search algorithm is binary search.
After the game is over, look at the bottom to see if you could guess the number
quicker than binary search! (Gray is your guess, Blue is binary search.)

Enjoy and good luck!
